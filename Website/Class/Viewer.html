Viewer

Constructor

public Viewer(double xLocation, double yLocation, double zLocation, int width, int height, int density, double[] frontDirection, double[] leftDirection, double screenDistance, ThreeDObject ... world )

The Viewer class represents a camera in your simulated world. Therefore it has an orientation, defined by the front direction and left direction, where front direction declares the general looking direction and left direction the rotation. X-, Y- and Z-Location are very self-explanatory the position of your Camera. So are the width and the height too. Density means the number of pixels per unit and the world represents the 3D-Objects of your scene. The only confusing Parameter is the screen distance, which is the distance from the ray starting point to your screen as outlined below.
